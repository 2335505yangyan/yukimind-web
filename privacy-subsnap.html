SubSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Local Financial Records

SubSnap is a utility tool designed to help you track your subscriptions. We believe your spending habits are your business, not ours. **All subscription data, costs, and renewal dates are stored exclusively on your device.**

2. Permissions & Usage

  • Notifications: Used only to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals.
  • Storage: Used if you choose to export your subscription records as a CSV file.

3. No Third-Party Selling

SubSnap does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not track which services you subscribe to or how much you spend.

4. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact: support@yukimind.com
