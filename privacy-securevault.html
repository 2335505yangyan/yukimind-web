Privacy Policy for SecureVault

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

SecureVault (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information. We operate under the brand **YukiMind**.

2. Zero-Knowledge & Local Utility

SecureVault is designed as a zero-knowledge local utility. We prioritize your data sovereignty above all else.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII).
  • User Files: All files (photos, videos, documents) imported remain exclusively on your local device. We have zero access to your content.

3. Android Permissions

To provide its core functions, SecureVault may request:

  • Media/File Access: To allow you to select and encrypt files from your device storage.
  • Biometric Hardware: To verify your identity locally via the Android System KeyStore.

These permissions are used strictly for local app functionality and are never shared or uploaded.

4. No Third-Party Analytics

SecureVault does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics), or advertising networks. Your behavior inside the app is private to you.

5. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all your encrypted files and app data by simply uninstalling the application or using the 'Clear Data' option in Android Settings. We do not store any of your data on our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@yukimind.com
