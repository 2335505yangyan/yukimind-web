Privacy Policy for AI ShotMind

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Developer: YukiMind Studio

Package: com.shotmind.ai.snapclean

Studio: YuKiMind Studio

1. Overview

AI ShotMind is designed with privacy as a core principle. All image processing and AI classification are performed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your photos or personal data on any external server.

2. How the App Works

AI ShotMind requests access to your photo library solely to read and analyze screenshots. All AI processing — including OCR (text recognition) and image classification — uses local models (Google ML Kit). No image data is sent to external servers.

3. Permissions & Usage

READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To scan and analyze screenshots.

ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION: To access full image metadata for precise cleaning.

4. Third-Party Services

AI ShotMind uses Google ML Kit (Text Recognition & Image Labeling), which runs entirely on-device. We may use Firebase Crashlytics for anonymous crash reports to improve stability, which contains no personal images or identifiers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy while using AI ShotMind, please reach out to us at: support@yukimind.com
