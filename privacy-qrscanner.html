Privacy Policy for ScanFast

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

ScanFast (by **YukiMind**) is built to be a high-performance, privacy-first utility. Our philosophy is simple: what happens on your device, stays on your device.

2. Camera Usage & Privacy

The app requires access to your device's Camera to function. However:

  • No Image Storage: ScanFast processes QR and Barcode data in real-time. It does not save, store, or transmit any images or video frames.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information, scan history, or location data.

3. No Cloud Services

ScanFast operates entirely offline for its core scanning functions. There are no backend servers, no cloud uploads, and no third-party analytics (like Firebase or Facebook SDKs).

4. Contact & Support

If you have any questions regarding this policy or the app, please contact our developer team at: support@yukimind.com
